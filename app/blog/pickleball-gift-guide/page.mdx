# The Ultimate Pickleball Gift Guide

*September 18, 2025*

Pickleball players are some of the easiest people to shop for — they literally never stop talking about pickleball. Lean into it. Here's what to get the pickleball fanatic in your life.

## Pickleball Shirts (Obviously)

A great pickleball shirt is the gift that keeps on giving. Every time they wear it to the court, someone will ask about it, and they'll think of you.

**Best for the funny player:** Look for shirts with slogans like "You Just Got Served" or "It's Always a Good Time." These are conversation starters that never get old (or at least, the wearer never thinks they get old).

**Best for the serious player:** UPF 50+ performance shirts are practical and thoughtful. They say "I care about your skin AND your game."

**Best for the fashionable player:** Premium tri-blend tees feel luxurious and look great both on and off the court.

## How to Pick the Right Size

When in doubt, go with the size they normally wear. Pickleball shirts should be comfortable, not tight — nobody wants to split a seam reaching for a kitchen volley.

## Gift Sets: Pair a Shirt With...

- **A pickleball shirt + a water bottle** — perfect for the player who's always dehydrated
- **A funny shirt + a serious shirt** — cover both their personalities
- **A shirt + a gift card** — let them pick their own second shirt

## Price Ranges

- **Under $20:** Funny graphic tees and basic cotton shirts
- **$20–$30:** Premium tri-blends and fitted styles
- **$30+:** UPF 50+ performance shirts and tank tops
