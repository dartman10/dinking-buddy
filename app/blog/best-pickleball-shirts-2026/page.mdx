# The 10 Best Pickleball Shirts in 2026

*March 29, 2026*

Whether you're a casual weekend dinker or a tournament-level grinder, the right shirt makes a difference — not just on the court, but in the parking lot afterward when everyone asks where you got it.

We've rounded up the best pickleball shirts you can buy right now, from hilarious slogans to serious performance gear.

## 1. Funny Slogan Tees

Nothing breaks the ice on a new court like a shirt that makes your opponent laugh before the first serve. Look for classics like "You Just Got Served" and "I Love It When My Wife Lets Me Play Pickleball" — the kind of shirts that get compliments between every game.

These cotton-blend tees are perfect for casual play, warm-ups, or just repping the sport at the grocery store.

## 2. UPF 50+ Performance Shirts

If you play outdoors (and most of us do), sun protection matters. UPF 50+ shirts block 98% of UV rays while keeping you cool with moisture-wicking fabric.

Brands like Fjalljós make pickleball-specific UPF shirts with designs like "Dink Power" and "Day Dink Vibes" — you get sun protection without looking like you raided a fishing catalog.

## 3. Premium Tri-Blend Tees

For the player who cares about fit and feel, tri-blend shirts (cotton/polyester/rayon) are the gold standard. They're softer than pure cotton, drape better, and hold their shape wash after wash.

The Dinking Buddy Premium Tri-Blend is our top pick in this category — it's butter-soft and the print quality is outstanding.

## 4. Women's Pickleball Shirts

The women's pickleball shirt market has exploded. You'll find everything from "Was That Out?" graphic tees to fitted performance tops with feminine cuts that actually fit well on the court.

Look for shirts with side vents or a slightly longer back hem — small details that make a big difference during active play.

## 5. Tank Tops for Hot Weather

When it's 95°F and you're playing your third game, a tank top is a lifesaver. Pickleball tank tops come in both performance and casual styles, so you can pick based on how seriously you take your sweat management.

## What to Look For

- **Fabric:** Moisture-wicking polyester for performance, cotton-blend for casual
- **Fit:** Slightly loose for range of motion — you don't want to fight your shirt on a backhand dink
- **Sun protection:** UPF 50+ if you play outdoors regularly
- **Design:** Something that makes you smile — pickleball is supposed to be fun
